About

I'm Alper Şahıstan a M.Sc. student and teaching asistant at Bilkent University Department of Computer Science . My areas of research include ray-tracing, volume-rendering, visualization and computer graphics. I am also interested in HPC and computational geometry.
Further Information
  • I am looking for PhD positions where I can do research on sci-vis, ray-tracing or computer graphics(Any pointers would be appreciated).
  • I'm currently remotely collaborating with Dr. Ingo Wald, Dr. Stefan Zellmann, and Nate Morricial on a 3D scientific visualization paper. Research is focused on compaction and hardware-assisted efficient traversal of unstructured meshes over multiple nodes. We have submitted a short paper demonstrating RTX-accelerated shell traversal for volume-visulization to IEEE VIS 2021.
  • We also utilized compact tetrahedral-meshes as acceleration structures for ray-tracing in a previous government-funded(TÜBİTAK) study supervised by Prof. Güdükbay Of Bilkent University.
  • I did my internship at Taleworlds Entertainment, where I developed C# scripts and c++ geometry extraction tools. My work was credited in the game Mount&Blade II: Bannerlord.
